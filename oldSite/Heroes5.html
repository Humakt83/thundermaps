Erythia
The realm of Erythea is in turmoil. Mirageans have contacted High Elves with intent of purging the world from evil. The Wizards of Thalark have summoned foul demons to aid them in the world conquest. Dark Elves and Lich King have formed an unholy alliance against the other races. And Dwarves are eager to battle anyone who threatens their borders. Or their gold.
